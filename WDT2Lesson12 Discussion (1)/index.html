Discover Portugal

Portugal is a country of beautiful landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. From the beautiful coastline to the historic cities, there is so much to explore.

Explore our website to find more about Portugal's stunning gallery, and dive deep into its culture and history.

What are some fun facts about Portugal?

  • Portugal is the oldest country in Europe.
  • The world's oldest bookstore is in Lisbon.
  • Portugal has had the shortest and longest reigning monarchs
  • Portugal's national animal is the Iberian wolf

Where is Portugal located?

  • Portugal is located in Southern Europe, neighborhing Spain.
